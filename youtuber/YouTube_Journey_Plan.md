## Your Husband Interview (Tonight)

**Why him first:**
- Safe, low-pressure
- 15 years together but genuine curiosity remains
- He models resilience/optimism you want to understand
- You have real questions (not generic content questions)
- Success = confidence boost for future interviews

**What you're actually exploring with him:**
- How does he stay optimistic despite pressure?
- What's his approach to stress/wellbeing?
- What can you learn from his mindset about resilience?
- How do you two connect more deeply?

**The Questions:**

1. "What would people who know you say is your defining characteristic? How do you see yourself?"
2. "You seem genuinely unafraid of difficulties. When did you realize that? Was there a moment that shaped that?"
3. "I've noticed you're optimistic even when things are hard. How did you develop that? Born with it or built it?"
4. "Your happiness seems consistently high even under pressure. What's the secret? What keeps you grounded?"
5. "Everyone has stress — what are your specific strategies for managing pressure? Cooking? Traveling? Learning?"
6. "You're curious about so many things. What draws you to learning? What's most interested you recently?"

**Then:** "What would you want people to know about your approach to life?"

**Setup:** Quiet room, phone voice memo, 30-45 minutes. That's it.
